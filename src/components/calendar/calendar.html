Calendar Component

Dashbase renders <calendar-view> into an interactive calendar surface with built-in month and year selectors. Use data-selected for single-date mode, data-mode="range" plus data-range-start/data-range-end for range selection, and add data-time when you want native time inputs alongside the calendar.

Feature-Rich Calendars

Arrow keys move day to day, Home/End jump across the week, and Page Up/Page Down move across months. The header selectors can jump directly to another month or year without a separate picker UI.
