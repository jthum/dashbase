Card Component

Dashbase cards use either a presentational <ui-card> host or a semantic host with class="card". The card shell is flush by default, so padded content lives inside <card-content> or native semantic regions. Use .simple for anatomy-free cards that just need shell padding.

Article Cards

Use <article> when the card is a self-contained piece of content that could stand on its own.
