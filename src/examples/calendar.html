Calendar Component

Dashbase renders <calendar-view> into an interactive month calendar. Use data-month to choose the visible month, data-selected for the picked day, and optional constraints like data-disabled, data-min, data-max, and data-week-start.

Standalone Calendars

Arrow keys move day to day, Home/End jump across the week, and Page Up/Page Down move across months.
