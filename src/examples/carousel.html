Carousel Component

Dashbase uses <carousel> when a sequence of slides should remain scrollable but still gain previous/next controls and optional dot navigation. The foundation stays native with scroll snapping; the behavior shim only coordinates state.

Feature Tour

Each slide fills the viewport width by default, which makes this a good fit for media previews, onboarding tours, and marketing storytelling.

Card Rail

Vertical Queue

Use .vertical when the carousel should snap on the block axis instead. Slides keep their natural content height by default, and arrow up and arrow down step through the queue.
